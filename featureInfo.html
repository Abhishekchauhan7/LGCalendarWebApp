Features

Comprehensive Calendar Views

Provides various options to display including year, month, week, day and agenda view. Move from one day, week, month or year to the next with a simple horizontal swipe.

Enhance your productivity

Keep track of appointment, task or event along with weekly weather information. Stay on top of tasks and priorities effortlessly.

Easily create event/task

Quickly create and share event/appointment with guest. You can also a reminder to ensure none of your things to do will be forgotten.

Simple Usage

Save time with easy navigation between various views.

Compatible with Google Calendar

Manage all your schedules at a glance by adding Google Calendar.

Customize & Manage your calendars

Enable/Disable option for weather information. You can set personalize color for calendars. Add & remove buttons provided for google calendars.
